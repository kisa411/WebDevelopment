So for theÂ past five days I've been away from Singapore and enjoying the lovely sights in Krabi, Thailand! I figured I would use the time for reading weeks to go traveling <3 Thailand is definitely one of the prettiest places I've ever seen and the locals are so friendly and kind :) They're all so innocent and always willing to help out if you don't ask them directly or even need it! :D It's really a wonderful place to visit and I don't think I can ever get tired of the sights and culture (especially the food! YUM) The place we stayed at (Sweet Home Guest House) had a great location because it was close enough to the night market and the Krabi river so that we were never too far from the central part of Krabi town but far away enough so that we were also able to have some quiet time if we wanted to. I think the best parts of the trip were the boat tour around the mangrove forest when we visited a cave and a fish farm (it was actually a seafood restaurant that grew its own fish from the river and prepared them freshly when ordered), the trek up the 1200+ stairs to the Tiger Cave Temple, and the 7 Island Tour and a night swim with glowing plankton <3 It was a great trip and now I'm feeling refreshed enough to start studying for finals! :)
