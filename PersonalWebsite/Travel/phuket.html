Krabi 2014

This weekend a bunch of some of my closest friends here and I went on a trip to Phuket, Thailand - it's an island off the western coast of mainland Thailand. I think I spent a total of $350 USD on the trip (Friday ~ Sunday morning) - most of the money went towards getting awesome (!!!!) Thai massages, and the plane ticket (of course), but the hostel we stayed at was only 14S$ a night which is really cheap for such a great hostel!! (We stayed at the Fin Hostel near Kata Beach, and the hostel is definitely worth checking out if you're planning a trip to Phuket <33 - the hostel owners are super friendly and helpful as well and they're willing to answer any questions you might have or give you tips on where to go if you're not sure).
