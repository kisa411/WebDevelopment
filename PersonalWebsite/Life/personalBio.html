The Life of Kohmily

More about me :)

I was born in Seoul, South Korea where I lived until I moved to Chicago in first grade. Since then I have moved around and lived in a bunch of different cities, including a couple of suburbs around Chicago, Portland, Irvine, Singapore, and Seoul. I currently live in Dallas, Texas.

I graduated from the University of Notre Dame with a degree in Science-Computing, which is a fancy name for our intercollegiate degree combining Biology and Computer Science. Right now I work at Call Box in Downtown Dallas as a full-stack developer. I'm new to the tech-industry and am using my time at Call Box to learn as much as I can about everything development-related and also new technologies!

When I'm not doing techie and development stuff, I love reading books, traveling (check out my travel logs here!), trying out new restaurants and visiting new places (keep updated with my local adventures :] ), and watching movies & cartoons (my favorite genres are animation and thrillers). I'm really excited to start this blog and use it as a virtual diary of my life, and hopefully it can be helpful to others in some ways :)
